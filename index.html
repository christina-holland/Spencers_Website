About Us

Welcome to Spencer Paws, where passion meets paws! Founded by a local youth with a love for dogs, we are a dog walking service in Echo Farms committed to providing top-notch care and companionship for your furry family members. Our journey began with a simple love for animals and a desire to make a positive impact in our community. At Spencer Paws, we understand the importance of exercise, socialization, and mental stimulation for dogs of all breeds and sizes. Whether your pup needs an early morning stroll while you get ready for work or an afternoon excursion while you're settling in and focusing on dinner, we here to ensure they receive the attention and exercise they crave. At Spencer Paws, we treat every dog like family. Contact us today to schedule a meet-and-greet or to learn more about how we can help keep your dog happy and healthy!

Our Features

Quality Service

We tailor our services to meet the individual needs of each dog, ensuring they enjoy their time with us to the fullest.

We are available early mornings between 6 AM and 8 AM and afternoons/evenings between 4 PM and 9 PM for your convenience.
